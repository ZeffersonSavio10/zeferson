About the Club

MORE THAN
A GYM

Lospalos Boxing Club was established in 2025 in Fuiloro Village, Lospalos — Lautem District, Timor-Leste. We were built to develop and support boxing athletes at every level: local, national, and international.

L.B.C is not just a training place. It is a home for fighters committed to transforming their lives through boxing and representing their community and country on the world stage.

Our foundation is built on four pillars: Discipline, Consistency, Hard Work, and a Champion's Mindset.
